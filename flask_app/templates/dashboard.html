How It Works

Hangry works to quickly provide you restaurant options to solve your hanger.

One option is to do a one-time use. With this option, Hangry will provide nearby options based on your location or the location you input. You can then select/deselect the options to narrow down your preferences. After, if there are restaurants that you wish to add to your options that were no previously provided, you can enter them manually. Finally, where your preferences are to your liking, you can proceed and Hangry will tell you where you should go! This is a good option for people who aren't familiar with Hangry's functionality.
