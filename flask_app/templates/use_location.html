What does Hangry use my location for?

Hangry only uses your location to search your area for nearby restaurants in order to provide you with suggests on places you might want to eat at.

What is the manual entry option?

Some users don't like to allow their browsers to access their location but Hangry still needs an address to provide nearby restaurant suggestions. For those users, we provide an option to manually enter an address. You can either enter your address or possibly the address of a nearby shopping center if you don't feel comfortable sharing your exact location.
